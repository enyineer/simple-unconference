Moderators can now "Refit rooms" on a planned slot: it reassigns that slot's rooms among its scheduled talks by popularity — the biggest room goes to the most-starred talk — while honoring pinned rooms and room requirements. It's all-or-nothing, so an unplaceable requirement surfaces as a conflict with no changes made. Attendees whose talk is scheduled into, moved within, or removed from a planned slot now get a coalesced "Schedule updated" notification (mandatory sessions notify everyone), and moving an unconference placement to a new room notifies the people currently seated there. The agenda response also carries a moderator-only participant count.
